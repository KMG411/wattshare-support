# Privacy Policy for WattShare

**Last Updated: January 30, 2026**

## Introduction

WattShare ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**WattShare does not collect any personal data.**

All information you enter into the app (properties, accounts, bills, meter readings) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

## Data Storage

- All app data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's secure data storage
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cloud synchronization is performed
- Your data remains entirely on your device

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Device identifiers
- Cookies or tracking technologies

## Third-Party Services

WattShare does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any data with third parties.

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- iOS device encryption
- Apple's built-in security features
- Local-only storage (no network transmission)

## Children's Privacy

WattShare does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have full control over your data
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- You can export your data using the app's export feature

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- GitHub: [https://github.com/KMG411/wattshare-support/issues](https://github.com/KMG411/wattshare-support/issues)
